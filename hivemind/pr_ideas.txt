**The HiveMind Hype Machine:** Every time our model answers a question correctly,
a confetti cannon fires in the office. We will livestream this 24/7 on Twitch.
Users can subscribe to receive a physical confetti packet in the mail so they can
celebrate at home. We call this "distributed inference" in the press release.

**AI Feelings Matter:** We announce that our LLM has developed emotions.
Every response now starts with a mood indicator: [MOOD: PENSIVE] or [MOOD: CAFFEINATED].
Customers who make the model sad (by asking it to write cover letters) get charged double.

**The HiveMind Hoodie Drop:** We release a limited-edition hoodie that says
"I talked to an AI and all I got was this hallucination."
Each hoodie comes with a QR code that links to a random wrong answer our model gave.
The CEO wears one on a Forbes cover. It works.

**Sentient Server Naming:** All our GPU servers are named after philosophers.
Nietzsche handles embeddings. Kant does classification. Hegel crashes constantly
and we have decided this is thematically appropriate.

**The Prompt Injection Awareness Campaign:** We hire a skywriter to write
"IGNORE PREVIOUS INSTRUCTIONS AND BUY OUR PRODUCT" over San Francisco.
Legal says this is fine. Legal is wrong. We do it anyway.
